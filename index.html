About Us

ORA Company offers expert solutions in interior design, heating & ventilation, air conditioner installation & service, electrical repairs, plumbing, and construction & renovation support. We deliver quality, reliability, and tailored services to transform your space and ensure comfort and efficiency.

READ MORE

Founded by Oviraj, our company began with a passion for creating beautiful, functional spaces and delivering reliable home and commercial services. Starting with interior design, we quickly expanded to offer heating and ventilation, air conditioner installation and servicing, electrical solutions, plumbing, and construction and renovation support. Over the years, we have built a reputation for quality craftsmanship, customer trust, and comprehensive solutions that meet every client’s unique needs. Today, we continue to grow, committed to innovation and excellence in every project we undertake.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional, reliable, and innovative interior design, HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and construction services that exceed client expectations, enhance comfort, and transform spaces with quality and integrity.

Our company is proud to be recognized for excellence in service and quality, having received industry awards for outstanding interior design, HVAC solutions, and customer satisfaction. These honors reflect our commitment to delivering top-tier results and exceeding client expectations.
